Solution Overview

Benefits for Patients

Immediate connection and assessment

We provide patients with access to the closest healthcare professional without an appointment.

You, as a patient, simply call or text using our app, and the closest available doctor will provide you with a quick video assessment and provide you with the next steps.

Referrals

If a referral is necessary, the doctor will be able to give you a referral to a qualified specialist immediately.

Doctor visits

If a doctor sees that video assessment is not enough he can visit the patient at their location to perform physical examination.

The doctor will assess you at your location for the appropriate next steps, possibly examining you, diagnosing, ordering tests, prescribing medication or referring you to a specialist.

Multilingual doctors support

We will try to match you with a bilingual doctor who speaks your language, so that you can describe your problem in greater detail, as well as better understand your treatment plan.

E-prescriptions

Doctors can access patients' profiles and write e-prescriptions directly to local pharmacies, which deliver medication to your door.

Medication Reminders

Our service provides easy-to-follow daily tasks, so that patients can remember what they must do to recover or support their health. This promotes patient involvement in their care plan.

Benefits for Healthcare Professionals

Joining the platform

Any registered doctor can join our platform and start engaging with their own network of patients as well as those outside of the network.

Time effective

Healthcare professionals can locate and communicate directly with patients and can save valuable time and reduce unnecessary visits by assessing people via video.

Flexible

Allows flexible workloads and schedules for doctors and nurses by allowing them to indicate their schedule in the system

Immediate Referrals

You can provide patients with referrals to other doctors, and other doctors will be able to refer patients to you. Patients will be able to communicate immediately with the right professional.

Covering your location

We provide the ability to see all local complaints in your area.

Medical professionals are able to start working flexibly from any location, treat patients nearby, access patient profile information, and write prescriptions and notes.

It improves the experience of communicating via video, audio or text, so you can react to patients complaints immediately.
